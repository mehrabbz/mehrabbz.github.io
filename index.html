§ 01

About

I'm a fifth-year PhD candidate in Computer Science at UCLA, advised within the intersection of ML, NLP, and health informatics. My research builds multimodal systems — text, image, and sensor data — for clinical and behavioral inference.

I design and train deep models (CNNs, RNNs, Transformers) and apply transfer-learning techniques (LoRA, RAG) to foundation models including LLMs and VLMs. My recent work focuses on Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF) and multi-objective preference optimization for clinically aligned language models.

I've completed industry research internships at Merck (NLP for drug discovery) and AstraZeneca (Graph-RAG over biomedical knowledge), and I'll be joining Google as a PhD intern in Software Engineering this summer.

§ 02

Research

Featured · 2026

Multi-Objective Alignment of Language Models for Personalized Psychotherapy

A multi-objective DPO framework (MODPO) that aligns LLMs across six therapeutic criteria — empathy, safety, active listening, self-motivated change, trust/rapport, and patient autonomy. Trained on a 335-person preference survey and validated by licensed clinicians.

  • 77.6% empathy
  • 62.6% safety
  • +17.2% over Gricean baseline
Project site → Code →
Ongoing

Reinforcement Learning for Clinical LLMs in Mental Health

Building a clinical preference repository and an RLHF toolkit for scalable model adaptation to psychotherapy contexts — with multi-objective DPO at its core.

2024 · Merck

NLP for Relation Extraction in Drug Discovery

PubMedBERT and BlueBERT for biomedical NER + RE on PubMed abstracts; GPT-4 few-shot with structured JSON output. Replaced the company's production extraction model.

2025 · AstraZeneca

Graph-RAG over Biomedical Knowledge

Prompt-based entity masking, co-occurrence weighting, and community detection to generate Cypher queries that dynamically retrieve attributes for an LLM-driven chatbot.

Ongoing

Risky Behavior Analysis in MSM Populations via Mobile Data

Built an Android (Kotlin/Java) data-collection app with AWS backend; fine-tuned LLMs on text and location signals to surface risk indicators for substance use and high-risk sexual behavior.

2021–2022

Mental Health Sensing via Smartwatch

Garmin (Monkey-C) + Android companion for real-time EMA capture; Firebase backend. Self-supervised contrastive pre-training on unlabeled wearable data.
